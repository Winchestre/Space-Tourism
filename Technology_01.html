Spaceport

A spaceport or cosmodrome is a site for launching(or recieving)spacecraft, by analogy to the seaports for ships or airports for aircrafts. Based in the famous Cape Canaveral, our spaceport is ideally situated to take advantage of the Earth's rotation for launch.
